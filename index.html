The Opportunity — NTSB
ntsb.gov runs on SharePoint Classic. That's the root cause of everything.

The National Transportation Safety Board serves survivors, safety professionals, journalists, and regulators. Its current platform — Microsoft SharePoint Classic — generates public-facing server errors, fails mobile users, and blocks content teams from self-service publishing. NTSB's Technology Modernization Fund engagement creates a clear procurement path. WDG and WordPress VIP eliminate the technical debt and deliver a site that works as hard as the agency does.
